DESIGN PRINCIPLES

PARC: Alignment

boeing.com

Two principles of alignment were used in this design. The edge alignment, which was applied on all the text and on the three middle images makes the content more balanced and organized. And the center alignment, which was applied on the rest of the images creates a polished and professional look.

Whitespace & Clean Design

uber.com

The whitespace principle used in this design made the website look neat, simple, attractive, and easy to the eyes. It creates harmony and balance, and enables the objects on the page to exist. .

PARC: Proximity

northropgrumman.com

The principle of proximity applied in this design helps visualize the different elements, reduce visual clutter, and makes the content more comprehensible. The elements are placed in close proximity to each other to establish a clear and close relationship between them.
