Target Audience

The target audience of weathercock.com is described below:

  • Who: Local residents and visitors of Fish Haven, Preston, and Soda Springs, Idaho.
  • Age: All age groups including children 12 years old and above.
  • Accessibility: The site can be accessed on any computer and mobile device through a web browser.
